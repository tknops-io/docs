# Welcome to tknops

tknops is an AI token cost management platform that helps you monitor, analyze, and optimize your AI token usage and costs.

## What is tknops?

tknops provides comprehensive tools to track and manage your AI token consumption across different models and providers. With tknops, you can:

- **Monitor token usage** - Track token consumption in real-time across all your AI operations
- **Analyze costs** - Get detailed insights into your AI spending patterns
- **Optimize spending** - Identify opportunities to reduce costs without sacrificing performance
- **Set budgets** - Configure spending limits and receive alerts when thresholds are reached

## Key features

- Real-time token usage tracking
- Cost analytics and reporting
- Multi-provider support
- Budget management and alerts
- Usage optimization recommendations

## Getting started

To begin using tknops, you'll need to set up your account and integrate your AI providers. Follow our quickstart guide to get up and running in minutes.
